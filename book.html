Our Rooms

Our water bungalow suites are the ultimate in luxury accommodations. Enjoy the tranquility of the Mediterranean in a private bungalow situated directly over the ocean. With its luxurious amenities and breathtaking views, a water bungalow hotel is the perfect place for a romantic getaway or an unforgettable family vacation.
